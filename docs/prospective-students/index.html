Prospective students¶

University of Rijeka students¶

If you are interested in expanding your knowledge from the courses through research experience in the research topics of the lab by participating in our present projects, get in touch via e-mail or come to 8th floor, room number 823.

Students from abroad (ERASMUS etc.)¶

We welcome ERASMUS(+) and other guest students who want to learn more about computational biochemistry using our computational laboratory and supercomputer facilities. If you are interested, get in touch via e-mail.
